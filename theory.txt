Escape Sequence or Escape Character:- They are special character which have some meaning.
And they are begin with backslash \
1) \n for New Line
2) \t for Tab (By default tab has 8 spaces)
3) \" for double quotes
4) \' for single quote 
5) \\ for backslash
6) \b for backspace (remove a character from its left)
7) \r for carriage return (means move the cursor at beginning of the line )
